PARAGON

This is PARAGON a speech to text system that alows users to record their voice. PARAGON will filter out filler words and recommend a more clean speech. To activate PARAGON, press the microphone button and allow it access to your microphone. Your recording will appear on the lft and the suggested speech will be presented in the right bubble.
